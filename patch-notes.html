Duck Dungeon Reboot Change logs

The change logs fof DDR.

Pre Alpha v0.8:

Patch Notes:
- New cursor texture
- New bullet texture
- Class system, different classes have different boosts to certain stats:
4. Default class, has no boosts to any stats
3. Knight, has a 40% boost to health but a 10% decrease in speed
2. Ninja, has a 20% boost to speed and a 30% boost to stamina, but has 20% less health
1. Healer, has a 20% boost to health but with a 20% reduction to stamina
